🚗 Car Escape Rush Hour

How to Play:

  • Drag cars horizontally or vertically to clear a path
  • Move the RED CAR to the exit (→)
  • Complete as many puzzles as you can in 60 seconds

Scoring:

  • Maximum: 10 rounds
  • Score = Number of rounds completed
  • Time limit: 60 seconds total

Controls:

  • Blue cars 🚙: Move horizontally (left/right)
  • Green cars 🚕: Move vertically (up/down)
  • Red car 🚗: Get it to the exit!
